How It All Begun??...

Tehile was born in Agust,2018 at Deliverance Church Utawala,when the church first introduced something called the Youth Week.
Many youths joined at that time because they were convinced it was a one week event... At that time,Tehile was known as the Youth's choir.
The name 'Tehile' came later on after the ministration when the group wasn't dismantled and the group decided to look for a suitable more youthful name.
That is how the name Tehile came through.
Tehile is drawn from a Hebrew name 'Tehilla' meaning High Praise or Glory.
As youths, this is what our lives should be like, offering God praises and giving Him all the glory.

What Is Tehile??..

Tehile is a home for many youths.
Most people have joined and they have found their voice in the society.
Tehile has brought many youths from different walks of life together,minister together, relate with another,help each other grow spiritually and vocally.
Tehile is all about sharing the love of God with each other and then pouring it out to the church at large.
Under the leadership of Pastor Emmanuel and Lucy Kokonyo, Tehile has gotten a lot direction and opportunities to minister in the church and even out of the church in times of high school mssions or college missions

Tehile's Mission Statement

To glorify Christ and make Him known through songs that shed light to His people in and out of Africa.

Tehile's Vision Statement

To impact generations and nations.
