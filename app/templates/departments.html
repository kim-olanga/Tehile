Reasons for having departments??...

For the sake of efficiency in any organisation there must be division of labour.
Where certain resposibilites are allocated to some people.
As I said earlier, Tehile is a home for many people and when all these people join, they come from diferent walks of life i.e educational backgrounds, social backgrounds etc...

How many departments are there??...
